Places


Nur-Sultan


Nur-Sultan has been the capital of the Republic of Kazakhstan since December 10, 1997. The city is located in the north of the country, on the banks of the Ishim River, administratively divided into 4 districts. Akmolinsk received the status of a city on May 7, 1862. Astana became a millionaire city in June 2017, when the city's population was 1,002,874 inhabitants


Burabay


Burabay is a climatic and kumysolechebny resort since 1910 in the Akmola region of Kazakhstan. It is part of the Borovskaya village administration. It was erected near the lake of the same name. Sanatoriums, mud baths and so on. The season is all year round.


Charyn Canyon


Charyn Canyon is a 154 km long canyon along the Charyn River in Kazakhstan. The canyon is located 195 km east of the city of Almaty, near the border with China. It is located on the territory of three districts of Almaty region: Uygur, Rayymbek, Enbekshikazakh
