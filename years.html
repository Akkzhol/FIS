Years

CHRONOLOGICAL TABLE OF THE MOST IMPORTANT EVENTS IN THE SUBJECT "HISTORY OF KAZAKHSTAN"
1. 2.6 million–12 thousand years BC - The era of the Ancient Stone Age.
2. 2.6 million–40 thousand years BC - Early Paleolithic.
3. 2.6 million–800 thousand years BC - the Olduvai period of the early Paleolithic.
4. 800-140 thousand years BC – Acheulean period of the early Paleolithic.
5. 140-40 thousand years BC – Mousterian period of the early Paleolithic.
6. 40-12 thousand years BC – Late Paleolithic.
7. 12-5 thousand years BC – Middle Stone Age (Mesolithic).
8. 5-3 thousand years BC – the New Stone Age (Neolithic).
9. 2 thousand–VIII century BC – the Bronze Age.
10. The middle of the 2nd millennium BC – The development of the production of bronze products by the tribes of ancient Kazakhstan.
11. VIII century BC ‒ VI century AD – Chronology of the Early Iron Age.
12. I millennium BC – The period of unions of the Saka tribes.
13. 209 BC – Formation of the state of the Huns (Huns).
14. 530 BC – The defeat of the Persian king Cyrus by the Sakas-Massagets led by Queen Tomiris.
15. V-IV centuries. BC – The period of the life of the "Golden Man" found in the Issyk mound.
16. 552 – Formation of the Turkic khaganate.
17. 704 – Formation of the Turgesh khaganate.
18. 751 – The Battle of Atlakh.
19. 870-950 – The years of Abu Nasir al-Farabi's life.
20. 960 – The proclamation of Islam as the state religion in the Karakhanid state.
21. 973-1050 – The years of the life of Abu Rayhan al-Biruni.
22. The beginning of the XI ‒ the beginning of the XIII centuries – The time of the existence of the Kipchak Khanate.
23. 1021-1075 – The years of Yusuf Balasaguni's life. 24. 1030-1090 – The years of Mahmud Kashgari's life.
25. 1103-1167 – The years of the life of Khoja Ahmed Yasawi.
26. 1465-1466 – Formation of the Kazakh Khanate.
27. The beginning of the XVI century. – The reign of Khan Kasym.
28. The beginning of the XVI century. – The creation of the laws "Kasym khanna kaska zholy" ("The beaten path of Khan Kasym").
29. 1538-1580 – The reign of Khan Khaknazar.
30. 1582-1598 – The reign of Khan Tauyekel.
31. 1598-1628 – The reign of Khan Yesim.
32. 1628-1652 – The reign of Zhangir Khan.
33. 1643 – Battle of Orbulak.
34. The end of the XVII century. – Creation of the code of laws "Zhety-Jargy" by Khan Tauke.
35. 1723 – The beginning of "Aktaban shubyryndy, Alkakol sulama" ("The Years of the Great Disaster").
36. 1726/1728 – The Battle of Boulantine.
37. 1729/1730 – The Battle of Anirakai.
38. 1731 – The adoption of Russian citizenship by Khan Abulkhair.
39. 1771-1781 – The years of rule of the Kazakh Khanate of Abylai.
40. 1783-1797 – National Liberation uprising led by Syrym Datula.
41. 1835-1865 – The years of Shokan Ualikhanov's life.
42. 1836-1838 – The uprising of the Kazakhs under the leadership of Isatai Taimanuly and Makhambet Utemisuly.
43. 1837-1847 – Uprising under the leadership of Kenesara Kasymuly.
44. 1841 – Opening of the first secular school for Kazakh children in the Bokeev Khanate.
45. 1841-1889 – The years of the life of Ibray Altynsarin.
46. 1845-1904. – The years of the life of the great Kazakh poet, thinker and educator Abai Kunanbayev.
47. 1913-1918 – Publication of the national newspaper "Kazakh".
48. 1916 – National liberation movement in Kazakhstan and Central Asia.
49. 1917 – Creation of the Alash Party.
50. 1917 – II All-Kazakh Congress in Orenburg. Formation of the government of Alash-Orda.
51. 1920 – Formation of the Kyrgyz (Kazakh) ASSR.
52. The 20-30s of the twentieth century – Mass famine during the years of forced collectivization. 53. 1920-1940 – Mass repressions.
54. 1925 – Transfer of the capital of KazASSR from Orenburg to the city of Kyzyl-Orda.
55. 1928 – The opening of the first university - the Kazakh State Pedagogical Institute.
56. 1929 – Transfer of the capital of the Kazakh ASSR from the city of Kyzyl-Orda to Alma-Ata.
57. 1932 – A group of figures of the republic wrote to Stalin about the scale of the catastrophe associated with the famine: G. Musrepov, M. Gataulin, M.Davletgaliev, E.Altynbekov, K.Kuanyshev ("Letter of five").
58. 1936 – The transformation of the KASSR into a Union (Kazakh SSR) republic.
59. 1937 – Adoption of the Constitution of the Kazakh SSR.
60. 1939 – The beginning of the Second World War.
61. 1941-1945 – The Great Patriotic War.
62. 1946 – Formation of the Academy of Sciences of the Kazakh SSR.
63. 1949 – The first ground-based atomic explosion at the Semipalatinsk nuclear test site.
64. 1954 – The beginning of the development of virgin and fallow lands.
65. 1956 – Introduction of universal compulsory seven-year education.
66. 1959 – Introduction of universal compulsory eight-year education.
67. 1960-1962, 1964-1986 – Election of D.A. Kunaev as the First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Kazakhstan.
68. 1961 – Launch of the first cosmonaut from the Baikonur cosmodrome.
69. 1978 – Adoption of the Constitution of the Kazakh SSR.
70. 1979 – Demonstration in Tselinograd against the creation of German autonomy in Kazakhstan.
71. 1986 – December events (Almaty).
72. 1988 – Rehabilitation of Sh. Kudaiberdiyev, A. Baitursynov, M. Zhumabayev, Zh. Aimautov, M. Dulatov.
73. 1989 – Creation of the ecological movement "Nevada-Semipalatinsk" headed by O. Suleimenov.
74. 1989 – Adoption of the "Law of the Kazakh SSR on languages", the Kazakh language was declared the state language.
75. 1991 – Flight into space of the first Kazakh cosmonaut T. Aubakirov.
76. 1991 – Closure of the Semipalatinsk nuclear test site.
77. 1991 – N.A. Nazarbayev was elected President of Kazakhstan in national elections.
78. 1991 – The Constitutional Law of the Republic of Kazakhstan "On State Independence" was adopted.
79. 1992 – State symbols were approved: Flag, Coat of Arms and Anthem.
80. 1992 – The entry of the Republic of Kazakhstan into the UN as a full member.
81. 1993 – Introduction of the national currency of the Republic of Kazakhstan – tenge.
82. 1995 – The Assembly of People of Kazakhstan was established.
83. 1995, August 30 – Adoption of the current Constitution of the Republic of Kazakhstan at a national referendum (Constitution Day).
84. 1997 – Transfer of the capital of the Republic of Kazakhstan from the city of Almaty to the city of Akmola (Nur-Sultan).
85. 2003 – The first Congress of Representatives of World and Traditional religions of the world was held in Astana (Nur-Sultan).
86. 2010 – Kazakhstan ‒ OSCE Chairman.
87. 2011 – Holding of the Asian Games in Kazakhstan.
88. 2017 – Holding of the World EXPO Exhibition.’
89. 2019 – Election of Kassym-Jomart Tokayev as President of the Republic of Kazakhstan.
